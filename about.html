About

Nails Lindes is a small, by-appointment pedicure studio in Spain, built around one idea, that foot care should feel calm, personal and never rushed. Every appointment is given the time it deserves, in a clean, warm space.

Behind the work is a deep love of detail and a medical orientation: spotless hygiene, premium products and gentle, corrective care. Whether it's a quick express refresh or a difficult, long-hidden problem, every case is welcome.
