Our services

Manicure

Shaping, cuticle care and a flawless colour or natural finish — classic, gel or a combination, always built to last.

Gel & builder

Added strength and length with BIAB and builder gels, sculpted to suit your hands and sealed for a durable, glossy finish.

Nail art

From a single subtle accent to a fully designed set — French, chrome, foil and hand-painted detail, made just for you.

Pedicure

Renewing care from heel to toe: a warm soak, exfoliation, nail and cuticle work and a long-lasting colour or gel finish.

Medical & corrective care

Beyond beauty, Nails Lindes offers careful medical and corrective treatments — medical pedicures, ingrown-nail care, callus and hard-skin removal, and support for problem or damaged nails.

Everything is carried out with clinical hygiene and a gentle, unhurried hand, so your hands and feet feel healthy, comfortable and well looked after.
