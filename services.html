Our services

Express pedicure

A quick refresh, soak, shaping, cuticle tidy and a clean polish. In, out and back on your feet.

Classic pedicure

Full pedicure: warm soak, nail shaping, cuticle and hard-skin care, finished with regular or gel polish.

Spa pedicure

Everything in the classic, plus exfoliation, a nourishing mask and a relaxing foot massage.

Deep, corrective foot care, core callus removal, heel-crack treatment and support for problem skin and nails, with no polish.

Medical & corrective care

Beyond beauty, Nails Lindes offers careful medical and corrective pedicure, deep treatment, ingrown-nail care, core-callus and hard-skin removal, heel-crack treatment and support for problem or damaged nails.

Everything is carried out with clinical hygiene and a gentle, unhurried hand, so your feet feel healthy, comfortable and well looked after. Every case is welcome, send a photo for a free consultation.
